What Payfin is

Payfin is a manual debt-payoff tracker and planner. You enter the debts you owe, log payments against them, and Payfin projects a debt-free date using the avalanche (highest interest first) or snowball (smallest balance first) method. It is a record-keeping and planning tool — it does not pay debts, move money, connect to your financial accounts, or negotiate with lenders.

Not financial advice

Payfin is record-keeping and planning software, not financial, tax, credit, or legal advice. Its projections — including the projected debt-free date, interest figures, and any comparison between strategies — are estimates calculated from the numbers you enter and the assumptions built into the app. They may differ from your actual lender statements. For decisions about your debts, consult a qualified professional.
