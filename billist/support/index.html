How do reminders work?

For each bill, Billist schedules local notifications 3 days before, 1 day before, and on the due date (at 9:00 AM your local time). They're scheduled on your device — make sure notifications are enabled for Billist in iOS Settings.

What's a "variable" bill?

If a bill's amount changes each month (like electricity), leave the amount blank when you add it. Once you've logged a payment or two, Billist shows an estimate based on your recent payments.

Is my data backed up?

Billist stores everything on your device only — there are no accounts and no cloud. Your data is included in your normal encrypted iPhone backup (iCloud or Finder). If you delete the app without a backup, the data is gone, since it was never stored anywhere else.

How do I restore my subscription?

If you've subscribed before — for example after reinstalling or on a new device — open the paywall and tap Restore. Your Pro access will be restored from your Apple ID.

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed by Apple, not in the app. On your iPhone, open Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions, select Billist, and choose to change or cancel. You can also go to the App Store, tap your profile photo, then Subscriptions. Canceling stops the next renewal; you keep Pro until the current period ends.

How is my privacy protected?

Billist never asks for a bank login and has no analytics or trackers. See the Privacy Policy for details.
