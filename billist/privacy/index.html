Billist

Privacy Policy

Effective June 12, 2026

The short version: your data stays on your phone. Billist is an offline app. It has no accounts, no backend, and no analytics. We cannot see your bills, your payments, or anything else you enter — that information never reaches us, because there is nothing for it to reach.

What Billist stores, and where

Everything you enter — bill names, amounts, due dates, recurrence rules, and your payment history — is stored in a local database on your device only. It is never uploaded, synced, or transmitted. Deleting the app deletes this data.

No bank connections

Billist never asks for bank credentials and does not integrate with any financial data provider. You enter and log bills manually; that is the point of the app.

Reminders

Bill reminders use local notifications, scheduled entirely on your device. No notification content is sent to any server.

Purchases

Optional subscriptions are processed by Apple through your App Store account. We use RevenueCat to validate purchases and manage subscription status. RevenueCat receives purchase receipt information tied to an anonymous identifier — never your name, email, or anything you enter in the app. Apple's handling of your payment details is governed by Apple's own privacy policy.

Analytics and tracking

None. Billist contains no analytics SDKs, no advertising, and no trackers.

Children

Billist is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this address with a new effective date. Because the app collects nothing, changes are unlikely to be interesting.
